Bio

I am Product Director at Sensyne Health with 10+ years experience in digital health and machine learning (AI). Ex-academic at the University of Oxford with a PhD in Signals and Biometrics, I am motivated by achieving large scale impact and transforming the ways healthcare is delivered. I have applied my knowledge and skills in creating innovative products that deliver clear, tangible benefits to clinicians and patients. I crave user and stakeholder engagement and love to distill clinical knowledge into great products and opportunities for innovation.
